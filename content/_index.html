Shining God's Light in the midst of suffering

The Work Volunteer

Who We Are

Anabaptist Refugee Committee is a group of Anabaptist Churches working together to help meet the need of refugees that have arrived in the United States. We work to coordinate groups of volunteers in situations where help is needed and also assist in working with communities where these people have resettled.

Our Vision

Esther 4:14 For if thou altogether holdest thy peace at this time, then shall there enlargement and deliverance arise to the Jews from another place; but thou and thy father's house shall be destroyed: and who knowest whether thou art come into the kingdom for such a time as this?

WHO? Conservative Anabaptist churches, working together with God, for His glory

HOW? Sending teams of volunteers into military forts, to help meet the needs of the refugees.

WHAT? Attempting to create relationships that will affect the lives of the refugees after they leave the forts and settle into a new normal.

WHY? To show the refugees the True Jesus, the Son of God, that gave His life for ALL men!

Our E4 Vision (Esther 4:14) is
Equip Anabaptists, to Engage with refugees, to create Enduring relationships, which Express the love of Jesus!
